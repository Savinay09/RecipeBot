RecipeBot Chatbot

After realizing that so many household food ingredients are wasted, we looked at potential ways to address this issue.

After reaching out to relatives and soup kitchens, we decided to build a chatbot. This idea was driven by our desire to simplify and streamline the user experience for getting recipes from scrap ingredients. Utilizing a hands-free platform like Google Home or Alexa would be perfect in this regard. Since upwards of 70% of all smartphones run on the Android platform, we decided to use Dialogflow to build our chatbot.
