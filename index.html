RecipeBot Chatbot

After realizing that so many household food ingredients are wasted every day, we looked at potential ways to address this issue.

(Check out our Vision for more info on the food waste problems we are facing)

We reached out to our relatives and soup kitchens, and based on their feedback, we decided to build a chatbot. The idea was driven by our desire to simplify and streamline the user experience for getting recipes from scrap ingredients you may have at home. Since upwards of 70% of all smartphones run on the Android platform, we decided to use Dialogflow to build our chatbot.
